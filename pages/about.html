SOME BACKGROUND.

This creator found herself growing up in the depths of the Pacific North West, where gigantic fluffy monsters are rumored to roam (sasquatch!). Within this realm of Earth, she had 6 siblings growing beside her. When it was time to leave the nest, she decided to go to the University of Washington, where she majored in communication and minored in entrepreneurship. During the fateful start of the Covid pandemic she stumbled upon the world of programming. She studied it on her own for a bit and eventually found herself applying to Ada Developers Academy. After being accepted, she quit her accounting job at a nearby hotel and truly immersed herself into the studies of programming. What called her into this world? Well, Ariel loves to work with technology while having the ability to be creative at the same time. Also, since the world around is increasingly more tech-involved, she wanted to get a better understanding of this world around her. She hopes to eventually work at a company that can help her grow further and has the same values as her.
