Background

Before joining Ada Developers Academy, Mia worked as a psychiatric nurse and was studying to become a mental health nurse practitioner. However, they realized that the lack of access to primacy mental health care is causing frequent relapse and hospitalization. Inspired by Dr. Tom Insel 's work in psychiatric research through modern technology, they made a transition into tech field hoping to increase accessibility of care for people in need.

Interests

Besides computer science and mental health, Mia also has a passion in music and performance art.
