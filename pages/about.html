About Me

Hey!!! My name is Devin Taylor and I was born and raised in Cincinnati, Ohio. I moved to Seattle, Washington in July, 2022 after being admitted into the Ada Developers Academy.

Before Ada

I attended Miami University, majoring in chemical engineering. While in school I taught myself how to do acrylic nails and other nail enhancments. Doing nails became a side hustle for me and I began doing friends' and associates' nails out of my dorm room. Under unfortunate circumstances I was not able to complete my schooling and decided to work towards getting my nail technician license while I figured out what I wanted to do next. After graduating nail school and getting my license, I started to work in a family-owned salon. I love doing nails and the creativity aspect of it but, I don't find it mentally stimulating enough for me. During the time I was working in the nail salon my sister was in her Ada process as a part of cohort 15. I learned more about Ada through her and learned that Ada could be a way I could get back into STEM.
