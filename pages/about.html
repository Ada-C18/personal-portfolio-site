Hi! My name is Katherine. My preferred name is Kat and I use she/her pronouns. My hobbies include taking care of my houseplants, mountain biking, and cooking. One interesting fact about me is that I met my husband in Spain on a study abroad trip when we were 19. I'm currently pivoting from the civil engineering field into software development. I previously worked as a water resourses project manager, where I specialized in riverine and storm drainage hydrology and hydraulics to support flood risk mitigation. I received my BS in Civil Engineering from Texas Tech University in 2014 and received my professional engineering license in the states of Texas and Colorado in 2018 and 2019, respectively.
