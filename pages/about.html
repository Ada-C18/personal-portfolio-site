Journey into JavaScript (& other dialects)

Before coming to Ada, I had a vision of what I wanted my career to look like. After a long time of reinventing, I realized that the vision I saw was one that limited my potential and sold myself short, but nonetheless, I saw my career as something to look up against. My fear of failure morphed into a barrier for success. While I'm still working on this, coding has changed my approach to how I believe in myself, and what I should believe I am capable of. Thanks to what started as a side hobby (JavaScript), I've found a passion and a sense of professional worth that I'm excited to develop. Anyway, we have a few fun facts and a small creative corner ahead. Enjoy!
