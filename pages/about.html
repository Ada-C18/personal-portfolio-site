Sunny is a coder who ponders much about the intersection of technology, art, ethics, and her role in a gloablized world.
Sunny received a bachelors in Women and Gender studies from San Francisco State University in 2019, and always strives
to be inclusive and create easy access for others in whatever she does.

Sunny's hobbies include cooking, exercising, making music, gardening, and spending time with loved ones. She is passionate about
financial literacy, technology, spreading kindness, and is dedicated to being a life-long learner. Sunny aspires to be the best version
of herself in all she does, whether that is as a software engineer, friend or human. She uses her values of positivity, growth, teamwork,
precision, empathy, and simplicity as guideposts in life
