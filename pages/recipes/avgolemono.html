Avgolemono

    Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs olive oil
  • 2 onion, small dice
  • 4 carrots, small dice
  • 4 stalks celery, small dice
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 Gallon chicken stock
  • 4 lbs chicken breasts
  • 2/3 box orzo or other tiny pasta
  • 4 lemons, juiced
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 bunch dill, minced
  • salt & black pepper to taste

    Directions

  1. Put olive oil in dutch oven over medium high heat. Cook onion, carrot, and celery until tender. Add a pinch of salt. Add garlic and stir until fragrant.
  2. Pour in chicken stock and bring to a boil. Plunk the chicken breasts in and return to a simmer. Cook until chicken is cooked through. Remove the chicken breast to a cutting board and shred with forks.
  3. While you are shredding the chicken pour the orzo into the stock and simmer until al dente.
  4. Squeeze the lemons and crack eggs into a bowl (or the liquid measuring cup). Whisk them together. Turn the stove off. While whisking the lemon and eggs, slowly ladle 2 scoops of the simmering stock into the mixture. Then while whisking the pot, pour the tempered mixture back into the pot.
  5. Stir in the shredded chicken and season to taste. garnish heavily with dill.
