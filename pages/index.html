Welcome to my website!

Thanks for stopping by!

My mission is to make cool stuff that exists somewhere between art and technology. The current state of mainstream tech is corporate and homogenous, run by cisgender men and driven by advertising and click counts. I believe there needs to be more creativity and room for genuine self-expression, especially in online spaces.

Through my work, I aim to empower misfits like myself to carve out their own space online and in tech. Together, we can build a better Internet and a better world.
