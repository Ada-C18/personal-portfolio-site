Gotchu

Gotchu is an iOS app that makes it easy for individuals to share and receive goods within their community. Utilizing a Google Firebase and a Swift iOS frontend, Gotchu provides a seamless and user-friendly platform for exchanging goods. Gotchu was developed from idea to full product over the course of three weeks.
