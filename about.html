About Me Soriano's Portfolio


Hi! My name is Diana Soriano, I was born and raised in Washington Heights, New York City, More commonly referred to the 'The Heights". My family is from the Dominican Republic and I spent my childhood summers and vacations there swimming in the majestic waterfalls hidden in the hills of the island. I hold a bachelor's degree in Business Relations from Baruch College in Manhattan and received my Master's in Urban Planning & Latin American Studies at New York City College. During my undergraduate years, I took time off from school to work at the Izmir Chamber of Commerce in Izmir, Turkey. This opportunity inspired me to follow a career in software engineering. I'm currently reside on Montauk, Munsee Delaware, Canarsie, Munsee Lenape Land.
